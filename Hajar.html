About Me

I’m Hajar Husseini, a board‑certified psychiatrist. My approach is holistic and collaborative—we work together to understand your goals and design a plan that supports sustainable wellbeing.

Areas of interest include mood and anxiety disorders, trauma‑informed care, ADHD, and perinatal mental health. I offer trauma‑sensitive, culturally responsive support for adults and adolescents.

How I Work

  • Comprehensive evaluation to understand your story.
  • Personalized treatment using therapy and, when appropriate, medication.
  • Collaborative goal setting and clear follow‑up plans.
  • Optional coordination with therapists, schools, or primary care.

FAQs

Do you accept insurance?

I am currently out‑of‑network. I can provide a superbill for reimbursement depending on your plan.

How long is an appointment?

Initial evaluations are typically 60–90 minutes. Follow‑ups are 25–50 minutes based on your needs.

Do you offer telehealth?

Yes. Secure video sessions are available when clinically appropriate and permitted by state regulations.
